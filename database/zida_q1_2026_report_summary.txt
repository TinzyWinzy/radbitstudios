Source: ZIDA QUARTERLY REPORT Q1 2026 (47 pages)

=== KEY METRICS ===
- New licences issued: 146 (down 32.2% YoY from 214)
- Total projected investment value (new licences): US$723.74m
- Total licensed investment value: US$1,924.68m
- Value change: -59.6% YoY, but +62% QoQ (fewer but larger, capital-intensive projects)
- Domestic Direct Investment: US$102.38m (+2,406% from US$4.08m)
- Licence renewals: +53% total, 22% timely renewals
- Capital structure: 46% equipment imports, 25% foreign cash, 22% foreign loans

=== KEY ACHIEVEMENTS ===
1. PPP Guideline approved by Cabinet - standardised framework for PPP preparation, appraisal, implementation
2. Fee reductions under SI 17/2026 (GI) and SI 18/2026 (SEZ) Regulations
3. BKPO Framework launched - positions Zim as outsourcing hub (93.7% literacy, 30k+ graduates/yr)
4. BKPO SEZ incentives: 15% flat CIT, duty-free capital goods, youth employment tax credit, 100% capital allowance
5. National Investment Single Window (e-Registration) Service Design Workshop with UNCTAD
