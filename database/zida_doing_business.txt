Doing Business in Zimbabwe — ZIDA

Access essential information for investing in Zimbabwe, including key investment opportunities, regulatory requirements, and practical guidance on establishing and growing a business. There are three ways to invest in Zimbabwe:

1. General Investment License: Establish your private business with ease. Investors can own up to 100% of their business. Provides flexibility and confidence to launch, operate, and grow your investment.

2. Public Private Partnership: Invest in partnership with the Government, local authorities, or state-owned enterprises to deliver infrastructure and public service projects that drive economic growth and national development.

3. Special Economic Zones: ZIDA oversees the designation, licensing, and regulation of SEZs. Investors can apply to develop or operate an SEZ, or establish businesses within designated zones.

BKPO (Business and Knowledge Process Outsourcing) Operations: Take advantage of Zimbabwes strategic location, skilled talent pool, reliable ICT infrastructure. Access step-by-step setup checklist and investment licensing guidance.
