ZIDA Tourism Sector — Investing in Paradise

Tourism is recognized as one of the key sectors of the economy alongside agriculture, mining and manufacturing. Zimbabwe national economic blueprints (NDS1 and Vision 2030) identify tourism as a priority economic sector. Zimbabwe boasts Victoria Falls (UNESCO), Mana Pools National Park (UNESCO), Lake Kariba, Great Zimbabwe Medieval Place, Eastern Highlands, Matopos (Cultural World Heritage Site), Hwange National Park, Malilangwe Wildlife Reserve, Great Limpopo Transfrontier park. Target: reach 2,579,974 arrivals (2018 all time high). Tourism receipts expected to increase from US million in 2015 to over US billion.
