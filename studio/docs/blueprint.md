# **App Name**: Radbit SME Hub

## Core Features:

- Authentication: User authentication with email/password and Google OAuth.
- Subscription System: Subscription management system with Free, Growth, and Pro tiers, gated features, and PayNow integration.
- Digital Readiness Assessment: A progressive 15-step form for digital readiness assessment, featuring state persistence and downloadable PDF reports.
- Dashboard: Dashboard providing AI-generated summaries and tips, radar and trend charts, and industry benchmarking for Pro users.
- Tenders & News: AI-curated tenders and news from user-provided URLs, with bookmarking, expiry alerts, and categorized articles. AI will be used as a tool to decide when an article matches a user query.
- AI Business Toolkit: AI-powered business toolkit that includes a profile generator, slogan generator, financial projector, competitor analyzer, templates, budget calculator, and exchange rate scraper. The tool will make complex analyses for business decisions easier to digest.
- Community & Messaging: A forum with real-time notifications for community engagement, direct messaging, and AI moderation. The AI tool will use algorithms to detect offensive behavior.

## Style Guidelines:

- For the Light Theme, the primary color is a vibrant greenish-cyan (#4DCAC6), inspired by the vitality and growth of Zimbabwean small businesses.
- For the Light Theme, the background color is a cool light gray (#F7F8FA). This color evokes modernity without being too distracting.
- For the Light Theme, the accent color is a darker shade of the primary, a teal-ish tone (#40A6A0).
- For the Dark Theme, the primary color is a bright cyan-green (#33D6C2), representing innovation against a dark background.
- For the Dark Theme, the background is a deep dark blue (#192633).
- For the Dark Theme, the accent color is a pure cyan (#00C8FF).
- Use 'Inter' (sans-serif) for body and headline texts, providing a clean and modern aesthetic suitable for business content.
- Employ a grid-based layout for dashboard and toolkit pages to ensure a structured and responsive design.
- Incorporate minimal animations with subtle hover and active states to enhance user interaction without being intrusive.
